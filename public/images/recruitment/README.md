# Recruitment Images

This folder contains all images for the recruitment pages.

## Folder Structure

- **hero/** - Hero banner images (full-width, ~1920x400px)
- **office/** - Office photos for image sliders
  - **hanoi/** - Hanoi office photos
  - **hochiminh/** - Ho Chi Minh office photos
- **training/** - Training and development photos
- **life-at-inno/** - Life at INNO photos
- **career-path/** - Career path diagram/photos
- **timeline/** - Recruitment process timeline image
- **qr-code/** - QR codes (Zalo HR, etc.)
- **awards/** - Awards and achievements photos
- **vision-mission/** - Vision, mission, core values images

## Image Guidelines

### Hero Images
- **Size:** 1920x400px (recommended)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Usage:** Page headers/banners

### Office Sliders
- **Size:** 1200x800px (recommended)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Quantity:** 5-10 photos per office

### QR Codes
- **Size:** 300x300px or 400x400px
- **Format:** PNG (transparent background)
- **Usage:** Contact information

### General
- Optimize images before upload (use TinyPNG, ImageOptim, etc.)
- Use descriptive filenames (e.g., `hanoi-office-main.jpg`)
- WebP format recommended for better performance

## How to Replace Placeholders

1. Upload your images to the appropriate folder
2. Keep the same filename OR update the path in the code
3. Images will be auto-optimized by Next.js Image component
